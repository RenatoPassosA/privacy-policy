It uses the WhatsApp Business Cloud API to simulate and study automated customer service flows.

The application may collect and process limited information such as:
- Phone number
- Messages exchanged with the chatbot

No data is sold, shared, or used for marketing purposes.

All collected information is used exclusively for learning, testing, and demonstration of software development concepts.
